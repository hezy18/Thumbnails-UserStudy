Video Experiment

Instructions / 实验说明

Experimental Instructions

This study consists of two parts: Module A (Interest Collection) and Module B (Thumbnail Selection). For this session, you are only required to complete Module A.

Steps

  1. Enter Module A: You will see two video sources: Vertical Videos and Horizontal Videos.
  2. Select Videos: Browse the selection page and pick videos that genuinely interest you.
  3. Watch & Rate: Please watch the video in its entirety before providing a rating based on your true level of interest. Please do not drag or skip through the video timeline.
  4. Skip Mechanism: If you find that you do not like a video while watching, you may stop at any time, provide a rating, and then return to the list.
  5. Target Count: Please complete the watching and rating for 20–30 videos for each source (both Horizontal and Vertical).
  6. Submit: Once finished, return to the module selection page and click the "Finish" button in the top-right corner.

Our Expectations

We aim to understand your authentic interests and viewing preferences. Your honest feedback is vital to our research — there are no right or wrong answers. Please interact with the content based on your genuine feelings.

实验说明

本实验分为两个部分：模块 A（兴趣收集）与模块 B（封面选择）。本次试验您只需要完成模块 A。

操作步骤

  1. 进入模块 A：您会看到两个视频来源：竖版视频与横版视频。
  2. 挑选视频：在选择页面中挑选您感兴趣的视频点击观看。
  3. 观看与评分：请将视频完整看完后，根据您对视频内容的真实兴趣程度进行打分。在观看过程中，请勿拖动视频进度条。
  4. 跳过机制：如果您在观看过程中不喜欢该视频，可以随时停止观看并进行打分，随后返回列表。
  5. 完成量：请在每个视频来源（横版、竖版）中分别完成 20–30 个视频的观看与打分。
  6. 提交结果：全部完成后，回到模块选择页面，点击右上角的 "Finish" 按钮。

我们的期望

我们希望了解您的真实兴趣想法和观看偏好。您的反馈对我们的研究至关重要，实验结果没有对错之分，请根据您的真实感受进行操作。

Module B — Instructions / 实验说明

Thumbnail Selection Experiment / 封面选择实验

You will see 20 video cases. For each case, please select the two covers you like the most and would most like to click on.

您将看到 20 个视频案例。对于每个案例，请选出您最喜欢、最想点击的 2 个封面。

Steps / 操作步骤

  1. Browse Covers: For each case, you will see 10 candidate cover images displayed in random order.
    浏览封面：每个案例会以随机顺序展示 10 张候选封面图。
  2. Select Two: Click to select the 2 covers that attract you the most. Your 1st and 2nd picks will be labeled with numbered badges.
    选择两张：点击选出最吸引您的 2 张封面，系统会标注您的第 1 和第 2 选择。
  3. Confirm & Watch: After selecting, click "Confirm" to proceed to a short video. Watch the video and answer one quick question about how well it matched your expectations.
    确认并观看：选好后点击"确认"进入视频播放页，观看视频后回答一个关于封面与内容匹配度的问题。
  4. Repeat: Complete all 20 cases, then click "Submit" to finish.
    重复操作：完成全部 20 个案例后，点击"提交"结束实验。

Our Expectations / 我们的期望

We want to understand which cover images genuinely attract your attention. There are no right or wrong answers — please choose based on your true preferences.
我们希望了解哪些封面图真正吸引您的注意力。答案没有对错之分，请根据您的真实喜好进行选择。

Video Experiment

Can you view and understand Chinese videos?

Instructions / 实验说明

Experimental Instructions

This study consists of two parts: Module A (Interest Collection) and Module B (Thumbnail Selection). For this session, you are only required to complete Module A.

Steps

  1. Enter Module A: You will see two video sources: Vertical Videos and Horizontal Videos.
  2. Select Videos: Browse the selection page and pick video covers that genuinely interest you to watch. You do not need to click every video in order.
  3. Watch & Rate: Please watch the video in its entirety before providing a rating based on your true level of interest in the video content.
  4. Skip Mechanism: If you do not wish to continue watching the current video, you may stop at any time and provide a rating, then return to the list.
  5. Target Count: Please complete watching and rating 10–15 videos each for Horizontal and Vertical, totaling 20–30 videos.
  6. Target Count: Please complete watching and rating 20–30 videos for Horizontal video.
  7. Submit: Once finished, return to the module selection page and click the "Finish" button in the top-right corner.

Our Expectations

We aim to understand your authentic interests and viewing preferences. Your honest feedback is vital to our research — there are no right or wrong answers. Please interact with the content based on your genuine feelings.

实验说明

本实验分为两个部分：模块 A（兴趣收集）与模块 B（封面选择）。本次实验您只需要完成模块 A。

操作步骤

  1. 进入模块 A：您会看到两个视频来源——竖版视频与横版视频。
  2. 挑选视频：在选择页面中挑选您感兴趣的视频封面点击观看，并不需要依次点击每一个视频。
  3. 观看与评分：请将视频完整看完后，根据您对视频内容的真实兴趣程度进行打分。
  4. 跳过机制：如果您不想继续观看当前视频，可以随时停止观看并进行打分，随后返回列表。
  5. 完成量：请在横版和竖版中分别完成 10–15 个视频的观看与打分，共完成 20–30 个视频。
  6. 提交结果：全部完成后，回到模块选择页面，点击右上角的 "Finish" 按钮。

我们的期望

我们希望了解您的真实兴趣想法和观看偏好。您的反馈对我们的研究至关重要，实验结果没有对错之分，请根据您的真实感受进行操作。
